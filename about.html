Cafayate

We are a people who respect their traditions. Throughout the year we work as a community so that the values inherited from our ancestors are respected and maintained from generation to generation. Some of the most outstanding traditions are: Patronal Festival in honor of Our Lady of the Rosary "La Sentadita" of Cafayate and "La Serenata de Cafayate"
